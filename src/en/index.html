About me

Not diets, but a focus on nutrient-dense food and comfortable workouts that deliver results through proper technique and addressing weak points. That is the system.

I came to nutrition and fitness through my own experience — when I realized that no diet works long-term if it doesn't account for your real life. Over 5 years of practice, I've helped more than 360 clients build a system that works specifically for them.

My approach is integrative. This means I don't work with nutrition or training separately. I build a program around your entire life: sleep, stress, schedule, habits. That's exactly why my clients' results are sustainable.

If you're tired of approaches that give results for a month and then everything comes back — you need a system, not another diet. And I can help.
